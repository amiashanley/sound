ABOUT

I am a location sound recordist and designer working across feature films, shorts, documentary, podcasts, and arts-based moving image. I work in a range of environments, from intimate dialogue and remote locations to controlled studio environments.

Standard kit: MixPre-6 II recorder/mixer, Sennheiser MKH 416 shotgun microphone, K-Tek boom, two wireless radio mics, and Tentacle Sync timecode. Additional equipment available as required.

Full kit list and rate card available on request.
