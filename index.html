ABOUT ME

I'm a sound editor, designer, and recordist with eight years of experience across film, podcasts, radio, installation, foley, performance, and composition. I collaborate with filmmakers, artists, cultural institutions, and creatives from diverse fields to produce detailed and purposeful sound.

I'm organised, attentive to detail, and focused on delivering high-quality sound—whether on set or in post. I’ve recorded in diverse climates and environments, and my current kit includes a MixPre-6, Sennheiser 416, Rycote Nano Shield Kit NS4-DB, Rode lavalier, Sennheiser EW100 G3 Radio Mic Kit, and a K-TEK KE-89 boom pole, with access to additional gear as needed. I work in Logic Pro X, Ableton Live, Adobe Audition, and Reaper.

I also work as a sound artist and researcher, teaching audio post-production, sound arts, and media studies in higher education and adult education. For my artistic and academic projects, publications, and talks, visit www.amiashanley.com.

Get in touch at amiashanley@gmail.com or @amias_hanley
