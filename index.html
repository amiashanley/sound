ABOUT

I am a location sound recordist and designer working across film, documentary, and arts-based moving image. I focus on capturing precise, characterful sound that supports the tone and rhythm of the work.

My approach is calm, collaborative, and attentive to both the technical and interpersonal needs of a set. I am comfortable working in varied environments, from observational scenes and small crews to studio-led productions.

Standard kit includes: MixPre-6 ii recorder/mixer, Sennheiser MKH 416 shotgun microphone, K-Tek boom, two wireless lavalier systems, and Tentacle Sync timecode. Additional equipment available as required.

Full kit list and rate card available on request.
