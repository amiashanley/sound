ABOUT ME

I'm a sound recordist and designer, with eight years of experience across film, podcasts, radio, installation, foley, performance, and composition. I work with filmmakers, artists, creatives, media organisations, and cultural institutions to record and design detailed and purposeful sound.

I've recorded in a wide range of contexts—from intimate dialogue scenes and high-altitude Alpine locations to controlled studio environments. Whether on location or in post, I’m focused on delivering high-quality audio that serves the story.

My standard kit includes:

1x Recorder / Mixer (MixPre-6)
1x Shotgun Microphone (Sennheiser MKH 416)
1x K-Tek Boom (KE-89)
2x Wireless Radio Mics (Sennheiser EW100 G4)
1x Timecode Box (Tentacle Sync E MKII)

I have access to additional gear as needed.

I also work as a sound artist, researcher, and educator, teaching audio post-production, sound arts, and media studies in higher education and adult education. For my artistic and academic projects, publications, and talks, visit www.amiashanley.com.

Get in touch at amiashanley@gmail.com or @amias_hanley
